Latin Fried Rice

Ecuadorian style fried rice is a delicious take on chicken and rice using veggies and eggs to give the rice a unique flavor. Goes incredilby well with ketchup as well. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Eggs
  • Peas Carrots and Peppers

Steps to Follow

  1. Cooke some rice in a rice cooker
  2. Make sure you clean the rice cooker and you cook for th appropriate time
  3. Add two tablespoons of salt for each cup of rice cooked
  4. Cook your eggs peas peppers and carrots
  5. Throw it all together and serve
  6. Add ketchup to taste
